Our Objectives
To make the human community healthy, conscious, and prosperous through health education, training, and health services by conducting the latest research on the existing problems and challenges in the health sector.
To coordinate with national and international organizations to solve the existing problems in the health sector by conducting the latest research related to health.
To make everyone aware of the indispensability of yoga education by informing them about yoga to stay mentally and physically healthy. Similarly, a healthy life, a healthy society, and a healthy nation are the need of the hour for children, young and old alike on the importance of yoga and sports and the benefits it brings to health. Explaining that special emphasis will be laid on the development of yoga and sports and the creation of skilled yoga teachers and athletes.
To run programs to provide access to quality health care to all by providing preventive, therapeutic, and managerial health services to the inaccessible classes and communities.
To run various programs to improve the existing health problems of infants, children, and adolescents and to support the construction of healthy citizens.
To publish health and education-related materials and reports and to conduct promotion programs through health education.
To conduct seminars, symposiums, workshops, and other programs to share the latest changes in the health sector, health-related programs, and experiences in coordination with national and international organizations.
To conduct programs related to environment and environment, drinking water and sanitation, and to conduct various programs to motivate the target group for a healthy lifestyle.
To conduct programs for the prevention of various types of communicable and non-communicable diseases and to expand the treatment and rehabilitation programs for the problems such as disability and paralysis caused by the effects of non-communicable diseases.
To conduct health education and training to bring positive change in the knowledge, perceptions, and behaviours of the health care providers and the community and to develop healthy behaviour in every person.
To provide technical assistance to provide quality health services to the community by conducting programs such as quality of existing health services, garbage management related to health services, strengthening of the health system, and other programs.
